Privacy Policy

What information do we collect?

We (SoftUmeYa) are committed to protecting the privacy needs of users. Pyonkee does not collect any personal information or usage of the application.

On Error Reporting

There is an email based crash reporter on Pyonkee. It is activated when a fatal error occurs on Pyonkee. We just collect core dump and process list (running programs other than Pyonkee), so the report does not include any personal information. If you care, you can also select not to send the crash report by just closing email window.
